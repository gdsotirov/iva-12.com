About us

"Iva 12-20" Ltd. is specialized in the production of loafs, bakery, cakes and pastry. It offers broad assortment of small sweets, loafs, pies and cakes. In all products are invested high quality materials.

"Iva 12-20" company is created in 1991 by Georgi and Veneta Belevi. Originally the firm produces refreshments and from year 2000 the production shifts to pastry. The firm operates in the region of Kardjali, Bulgaria, but has established trading relations with neighbor regions.
